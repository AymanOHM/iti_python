Exercise 1: "About Me" Page

Create a page named aboutme.html that describes you. (You can use DreamWeaver or Notepad++). On your page, include some or all of the following information:

  • Your name
  • A description of yourself in two sentences or less. Emphasize the most important word(s) by putting them in bold.
  • A list of classes you are taking right now at Temple.
  • Your favorite movies, books, or TV shows, in order. Make at least one link to an interesting site about that movie/book, such as its IMDB page.
  • Two images, one that represents you now, one that represents you when you were younger.
  • Something interesting about one or more of your neighbors (people sitting at computers next to you).

(To see your page in the web browser, in Firefox, click File, Open File... and browse to your page to open it.)

Write your page using valid XHTML 1.1. Include a link from your page to the W3C XHTML Validator, using the following image and linking to the following URL:

  • image: http://www.w3.org/Icons/valid-xhtml11
  • site: http://validator.w3.org/check?uri=referer

Also include a link to the W3C CSS Validator (image and URL):

  • image: http://www.w3.org/Icons/valid-css
  • site: http://jigsaw.w3.org/css-validator/check/referer
